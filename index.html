About

Engineer. Builder. Inventor.

I'm an Embedded Systems & Robotics Engineer who builds autonomous machines from the ground up — from hand-soldered PCBs and 3D-printed chassis to LQR controllers and edge AI pipelines running on Raspberry Pi.

Currently finishing my M.Sc. in Electronic Embedded Systems at ESIGELEC, France. Previously led a team building gesture-controlled drones and IoT humanoid robots at Learning Links Foundation, Mumbai.

I hold a granted patent for a bionic prosthetic arm, two published research papers, and 8 competition wins at IIT Bombay, IIT Ropar, LNMIIT Jaipur, and Punjab Engineering College.
